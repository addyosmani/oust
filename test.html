What are The Mobileys?

The Mobileys, produced by Mobile Future, is an annual competition that challenges innovators across the U.S. to create game changing apps, products, and services that use mobile to make a difference.

This annual national competition recognizes and supports early-stage mobile entrepreneurs who make the world a better place.

The Criteria

Early Stage

Applicants should be an individual or be affiliated with a small U.S. company that has either produced a mobile product or app or is within 6 months of launching a mobile product or app.

Game Changer

This product should be a creative game changer that uses mobile in new ways to have a positive impact on our lives.

Demoable

Winners must be able to demo their innovation in November 2016 at an event announcing the winners.

Seeking Capital

A little money can go a long way. The Mobileys prize money should help the winners expand, grow or develop another new game-changing mobile innovation.

Wireless

The nominated product must run on wireless networks (not just wi-fi).

Previous Winners

Our past competitions have celebrated some of the nation's brightest tech innovations and we are proud to showcase past winners of The Mobileys below.

2015

First Place

CureSearch CancerCare

CureSearch CancerCare is a mobile app that manages a child's treatment and advances parents' ability to share crucial information and reports.

Second Place

SPROKIT

SPROKIT is a mobile app that reduces recidivism by better connecting returning citizens with their parole agents and probation officers.

Third Place

Pro:Up

Pro:Up is a mobile app that matches high school students to educational and career experiences they need to ignite their passions and define their careers.

People's Choice

GiftAMeal

GiftAMeal is a mobile app that donates a meal to someone in need through Operation Food Search every time a user dines at a partnered restaurant.

2014

First Place

Code Blue

Code Blue is a mobile app that helps teenagers and young people experiencing depression or bullying get immediate support.

Second Place

First Derm

First Derm is a mobile app that provides both consumers and businesses a faster, more convenient means to receive professional opinion on skin care.

Third Place

VolunteerMark

Volunteer Mark is a mobile app that makes it easy to find the perfect volunteer opportunity based on skills, interest, availability and location.

People's Choice

First Derm

First Derm is a mobile app that provides both consumers and businesses a faster, more convenient means to receive professional opinion on skin care.

2013

First Place

Page-Out

Page-Out allows emergency responders the ability to page-out when not available from their smartphones.

Second Place

HeadsUP!

HeadsUP! is a new transparent display that integrates with your smartphone to display notifications on the windshield without obstructing the driver's view.

Third Place

Learn with Homer

Learn with Homer is a comprehensive reading app built by literacy experts for the iPad that offers a complete Pre-K and K reading curriculum.

People's Choice

INRFOOD

INRFOOD is a personalized nutrition platform that scans the bar code of over 300,000+ products, and instantly analyzes the nutrition facts and ingredients.
